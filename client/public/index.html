Our Mission

Our goal is to make a community for people who are focused on improving themselves physically. Whether you are a veteran body builder or a complete novice, Spot Me is here to connect you to others who share your vision. If you need some advice go to the live chat room and ask somebody, if you want to record your fitness progress write an entry in your fitness journal! Keep in touch with others in your area and chat it up, who knows you may see them at your gym! And if you ever need a workout partner just tell them Spot Me!
